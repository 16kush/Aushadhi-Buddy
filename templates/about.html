About Aushadhi Buddy

Aushadhi Buddy is a friendly chatbot that helps you identify your symptoms and provides predictions on potential health conditions based on the symptoms you describe. The chatbot uses advanced algorithms and machine learning to provide accurate predictions and suggestions for medical conditions. Aushadhi Buddy aims to make healthcare information more accessible and assist users in seeking appropriate medical advice.

Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of individuals by leveraging technology and data-driven solutions. Aushadhi Buddy is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is essential to consult with qualified healthcare professionals for personalized medical guidance.
